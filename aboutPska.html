Pierian Spring Kiddies Academy

Pierian Spring Kiddies Academy was founded in September 1996 with the sole aim of giving qualitative education to children. Our philosophy of education is encapsulated in our adoption of Alexander pope's immortal lines, both as school name and motto.We believe a house is as strong as its foundation.
The school currently has a population of about 400 pupils and a staff strength of seventeen. We offer variety of subjects which we believe are essential for our pupils at this stage of their development. Presently we teach English language, Yoruba Language, Mathematics, Computer Science, Music, Elementary Science, Agricultural Science, Social Studies, Writing, Physical and Health Education, Rhymes, Religious knowledge.

Vision

To create one of the most effective schools that prepares students for post secondary education, and also the world at large.

Mission Statements

  • To provide a godly, welcoming, safe, happy school where everyone is loved, respected an listened to.
  • To vreate a school(learning) community where children participate, excel, and are proud of their achievements.
  • To enable (equip) students to become confident, independent and successful learners.
  • To strive for high standard of achievement and behaviour.
  • To provide programmes which enable our students to become active citizens of the future.
  • To create a school that is highly regarded for its academic excellence, and for its contribution in serving and improving the community(society) in which it opereates.
  • To be commited to working in partnership with our parents and wider community.
